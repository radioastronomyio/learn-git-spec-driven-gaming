Smash the Galaxy

Bounce the energy orb. Shatter every brick. Catch power-ups. Don't lose your lives.

How to Play

◆ Drag, A/D, ← →, or gamepad to move the paddle.

◆ Click / Tap / Space / A button to launch the ball or fire lasers.

◆ Hit position on paddle controls bounce angle.

◆ Cyan bricks: 1 hit. Purple: 2. Pink: 3.
◆ Red bricks explode and chain to neighbors.
◆ Mystery bricks drop a random power-up.
◆ Boss bricks have heavy HP — drop big rewards.

◆ Catch falling capsules: Multi-Ball, Wide Paddle, Sticky, Laser, Slow, +Life, ×2 Score.
◆ Avoid Narrow Paddle (red).

◆ Chain bricks without missing for a Combo Multiplier.
